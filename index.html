About Us

Who We Are

Topline Stores Hammel is your friendly, community-oriented general store located in Kilmuckridge, Co. Wexford, Ireland. We pride ourselves on being a convenient one-stop-shop for our local community, offering a wide range of essential products and services.

From daily groceries to essential hardware and household goods, we are dedicated to serving the needs of our customers with a smile. We also provide vital Post Office services, making us a true local hub.
